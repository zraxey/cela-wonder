What are peptides, and how do they differ from drugs or supplements? +
Peptides are short amino acid chains that your body naturally produces, but levels decline with age and stress.

Unlike drugs, they don't force the body into action. Instead, they work through signaling — stimulating processes your body already performs to work more efficiently. Unlike supplements, they have specific structures and far more targeted effects.
Do I have to inject at home? Is it difficult or painful? +
Most injections are subcutaneous (SC) in the abdomen / thigh / upper arm / upper glutes (outer upper hip) using a 31-32G insulin needle — far less painful than a blood draw. Rotate injection sites each time to prevent tissue hardening.

After 2-3 injections, most members can do it comfortably on their own. The Cela Wonder team is ready to guide you through every step, and there is a detailed injection guide on the Recon Guide page.
Which peptide should I start with? +
It depends on your primary goal:

Weight loss / fat loss: Tirzepatide — the best entry point, good tolerance, great value
Skin & hair: GHK-Cu — very safe, visible results within 4 weeks
Sleep / stress: DSIP or Selank — can be used on-demand
Muscle / recovery: CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin — classic entry for body recomp

How long until I see results? +
It varies by type:

GLP-1 (Tirz / Reta): Appetite reduction within 2-4 days; weight loss begins at weeks 2-3
GHK-Cu / KLOW: Visible skin and hair results in 3-6 weeks
CJC + Ipamorelin: Better sleep within 1-2 weeks; improved recovery in 2-3 weeks
DSIP / Selank: Some people notice a difference from the very first night

Titrating your dose as recommended helps you get results faster and more safely.
Is it safe? What side effects should I know about? +
Peptides have a strong safety profile because the body recognizes their structure. Common side effects include:

GLP-1 (Tirz / Reta): Nausea during dose titration — resolved by injecting before bedtime
GH Secretagogues: Mild water retention initially — resolves on its own in 1-2 weeks
NAD+: Hot flush if injected too fast — resolved by injecting slowly over 30-60 seconds

Key principle: start with a low dose, titrate slowly, and always consult the Cela Wonder team before increasing or stacking a new peptide.
